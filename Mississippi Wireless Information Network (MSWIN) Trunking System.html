System Notes

In April 2005, the State of Mississippi committed to the building of a statewide interoperable public safety radio system, now known as the Mississippi Wireless Information Network (MSWIN). Section 25-53-171, Mississippi code of 1972, established the Mississippi Wireless Communication Commission (WCC) as the entity responsible for overseeing and maintaining effective public safety emergency communications in the State of Mississippi. MSWIN is currently utilized by almost all state agencies as well as a majority of counties and local agencies, with more and more joining each year.
